“Who are we?”
In early 2021, Aura Flora launched a comprehensive e-commerce platform, providing prime fresh-cut imported flowers arranged by a professional team of florists and designers. Aura Flora is leading the way in Egypt by providing a wide range of special flowers, chocolates, perfumes, silver, gold, candles, electronics, skin care products, watches and mixed bundles
More than just an online flower shop, Aura Flora aims to deliver a unique shopping experience to its clients with just a few clicks.
“What sets us apart?”
At Aura Flora, we're dedicated to simplifying the process of sending flowers and gifts, ensuring it's as enjoyable as receiving them.
Every day, we carefully select the finest flowers from global farmers to create stunning arrangements for any occasion. Plus, we offer a diverse range of gifts through partnerships with local and international brands, including chocolates, perfumes, cakes, jewelry, and more. We import high-quality flowers from Kenya, Ecuador, and the Netherlands, while our design team stays updated on the latest trends.
Each flower delivery is handled with care, arriving fresh and beautiful. We accept various payment methods for your convenience.
Our website and app are user-friendly, making it easy to find the perfect gift.
We're always adding new products to keep things exciting for our customers.
